HOW MANY YEARS OF EXPERIENCE DOES YOUR FIRM HAVE?

We started back in 2018 and have served millions of customers all over the world. We have gained first-hand experience dealing with customers and therefore have more intel about our customers wishes.

DO YOU DELIVER EYEWEAR PRODUCTS OUTSIDE KENYA?

We partner with other global organizations and ensure that your eyewear product is delivered anywhere our customers are located globally.

DO YOU OFFER COMPESATION AFTER BREAKAGES?

We ensure good packaging of our products and carefully handling during and after delivery so that the product reaches to its respective customer in one piece. In case of breakages during this process, we offer full compensation of glasses. However, if breakages occur after delivery in customers care, compensation will not be considered.

WHERE ARE YOUR STORES LOCATED?

We currently have two stores located in Egerton University Gate, Njoro and the other in CBD Nakuru city in Kenya. We also deliver glasses locally in Kenya and also globally where our customers are located. You are welcome at our stores today.
